Car accident
risk & pricing model

A frequency–severity model built on Poisson GLM, Gamma claim severity, and Bühlmann credibility theory. Pure premium output with full relativity decomposition and sensitivity analysis.

Launch app
or run streamlit run car_risk_app_v2.py
Model components
Frequency
Poisson GLM
Log-linear multiplicative relativities. Mileage treated as exposure offset. Outputs expected claim rate λ and full PMF.
Severity
Gamma GLM
Right-skewed positive distribution. Constant CV assumption. Outputs mean severity, P80, P95 via CDF and full density curve.
Pricing
Pure premium
E[N] × E[S] under independence. Loaded to gross indicated premium via expense and profit margin.
Credibility
Bühlmann weighting
Z = n/(n+k), k=4. Blends individual λ with population mean as policy years on file accumulate.
